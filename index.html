Services

From tired, gray asphalt to deep‑black, smooth driveways — we handle prep, repair, and finishing so you don’t have to.

Resurfacing
Driveway resurfacing

For driveways that are structurally sound but worn, we apply a new asphalt layer to restore a smooth, even surface and rich black finish.

Ideal for: aged asphalt, light cracking, uneven wear.

Protection
Asphalt sealcoating

A protective seal that shields your driveway from sun, salt, oil, and water — extending the life of your asphalt and keeping it looking new.

Ideal for: newer or resurfaced driveways needing protection.

Repair
Crack filling & patching

We address cracks and problem spots before they spread, helping prevent water intrusion and further damage beneath the surface.

Ideal for: visible cracks, small potholes, edge breakdown.

Our process

A simple, transparent process from estimate to final walkthrough — so you always know what’s happening on your driveway.

Step 1
Walkthrough & estimate

We look at your driveway in person, talk through options, and give you a clear, written estimate.

Step 2
Prep & repairs

Cracks, edges, and problem areas are addressed first so your new surface has a solid foundation.

Step 3
Resurfacing / sealcoating

We apply the appropriate material for your driveway’s condition — resurfacing, sealcoat, or both.

Step 4
Cure & final check

We mark off the area, review cure times with you, and do a final walkthrough before we call it done.
